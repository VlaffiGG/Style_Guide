Color Usage

Blue

  • Evokes a feel of confidence, trust and intelligence
  • Makes websites look clean, modern and cool
  • Good for finance, agency, portfolio or buisness

Pink

  • Acts soothing, calming and neutral
  • Can also be passionate and strong
  • Stands for creativity and cheerfulness

Green

  • Looks reliable, safe and eco-friendly
  • Works very well for buttons
  • Perfect for tourism, medication or health

Beige

  • Wonderful as a background color
  • Makes the users feel comfy and at ease
  • For architecture, fashion and cosmetics

Red

  • Evokes emotions of energy, speed and passion
  • Can work even better for buttons than green
  • Should be used to indicate interactability

Purple

  • Contrasts well with white, e.g. behind text
  • Associated with creativity or luxury
  • Makes sites look unique and memorable
